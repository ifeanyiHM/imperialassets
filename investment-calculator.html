Investment Calculator

Use this calculator to estimate the potential growth of your investment over time. Simply enter the initial investment amount, expected annual interest rate, and the duration of your investment. The calculator will display your estimated returns, considering the effects of simple interest.

Please note that the figures are indicative and actual returns may vary depending on market conditions, fees, and taxes. This tool is meant to provide a general understanding of how your investment might grow, helping you make informed financial decisions .
